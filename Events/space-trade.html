Space Trade - A Shark Tank

Description:

This event is a single-round event. The participants need to present thier business idea. The participants need to submit their ppt two days before the start of the event. It shows entrepreneurs making business presentation to a panel of “sharks”. The best business module will be announced as winners.


Rules

  1. Team size: minimum of 1 member and maximum of 3 members.
  2. Participants should submit their business proposal with a presentation of maximum 5 to 10 minutes, The slides presented should contain the equity shares.
  3. Kindly bring your PowerPoint presentation on a pen-drive and have a copy in your mail.
  4. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
  5. The ppt needs to be submitted 2 days before the event through google forms attached below.
  6. Only the selected team needs to present their presentation on the day of the event.
  7. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained
