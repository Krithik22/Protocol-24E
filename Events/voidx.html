VoidX

Description

This is a single round event. The participants will be given a time limit of 2 hours to complete their front-end design and the Theme will be given at the beginning of the event. The team with attractive UI/UX will be announced as winners.

Rules

  • Team size: minimum of 2 members and maximum of 3 members.
  • Participants can use any tool to develop a web design preferably figma.
  • Kindly bring your laptop on the day of event.
  • The topic will be given on the day of event.
  • Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
  • The participants are required to have basic knowledge in design.
  • Plagiarism not allowed.
  • The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained
