VoidX - A UI/UX

Description:

This is a single round event. The participants will be given a time limit of 2 hours to complete their front-end design and the Theme will be given at the beginning of the event. The team with attractive UI/UX will be announced as winners.


Rules

  1. Team size: minimum of 2 members and maximum of 3 members.
  2. Participants can use any tool to develop a web design preferably figma.
  3. Kindly bring your laptop on the day of event.
  4. The topic will be given on the day of event.
  5. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
  6. The participants are required to have basic knowledge in design.
  7. Plagiarism not allowed.
  8. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained
