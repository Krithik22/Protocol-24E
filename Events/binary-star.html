Binary Star - A Pair Programming

Description:

This event is a one-round event with 6 slots. This event needs spontaneous capability of adaptability to their partner. Once one round gets over, the other participant in the group has to continue from where the 1st participant left programming. The team which finishes first will be announced as winners.


Rules

  1. Team size: minimum 2 members.
  2. One minutes of opt time will be given.
  3. The participants are required to have basic knowledge in programming.
  4. All the programming languages like C, C++, Java, Python, etc are allowed.
  5. Helping the other team member during his/her slot time is not allowed.
  6. Participants must have a HackerRank account before the commencement of the event.
  7. Persons from different institutions can be a part of the same team. However, one person may not be a part of multiple teams for the same event.
  8. Discussion among participants is not encouraged.
  9. The decision of judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
