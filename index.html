About Us

  • Protocol 24e is a two-day technical symposium hosted by the Department of Information Technology and the Association of Information Technologists at SVCE. The event is designed to provide a platform for students to showcase their technical skills, knowledge and creativity through a variety of competitions, workshops and talks.

  • At Protocol 24E, participants will compete in various different events, including coding competitions, design challenges, and technical quizzes, to win amazing rewards and prizes.Participants will have the opportunity to work in teams or individually to demonstrate their skills and knowledge in these various fields.

  • Participants will have the chance to win exciting prizes for their performances in the various events, including cash prizes, certificates, and other rewards. Protocol 24E is the perfect opportunity for students to showcase their talent and creativity, and to learn from some of the best in the field.

  • Protocol 24E is an excellent opportunity for students to learn, grow and connect with other like-minded individuals in the field of Information Technology. We look forward to welcoming all the participants and wish them the best of luck in their respective events.
